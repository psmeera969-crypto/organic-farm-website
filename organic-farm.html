How It Works

From seed to your doorstep

Every step of our process honours the land and the people who tend it.

🌱
Sow with Intention
Seeds selected for flavour and resilience. Planted in composted, mineral-rich soil at the right lunar phase.
💧
Nurture Naturally
Drip irrigation from our rainwater tanks. Companion planting keeps pests away — no chemicals needed.
🧺
Harvest at Dawn
Picked by hand at peak ripeness. Sorted, cleaned, and packed within hours to lock in freshness.
🚚
Delivered Fresh
Same-day or next-morning delivery to your door. Packed in compostable jute and banana-leaf wrapping.
